An effective studying tool that's actually free!

Unlike other apps and websites that pretend to be free, Quizfreely is free. Other digital flashcards and study tools have free trails with time limits, annoying ads and pop-ups, or features that cost money or require an account. Quizfreely has the same effective studying methods, without any free trails, ads, or paid features. It's fully free and doesn't even require an account!

Free

Quizfreely is completely free. It does NOT require an account and does NOT have any extra paid features.

Open Source

Quizfreely is free and open source. It's NOT owned or funded by any company.

No Ads

Quizfreely does NOT have any ads or annoying pop-ups.

It's open source!

Quizfreely is open source software. Which means it's developed publicly, where anyone can view, critique, or contribute to the project's source code. Quizfreely's source code is hosted on Codeberg; a non-profit git hosting platform that is also open source!

It's safe!

Quizfreely uses HTTPS, so you can be sure your connection is secure. Quizfreely does NOT use browser cookies to track its users and does not sell users' data to anyone!

Accounts?

Quizfreely does have accounts. However, they're completely optional and all of Quizfreely's features can be used without them! The only difference is that users with accounts can save study sets to their account, instead of having to download them. With and without an account, Quizfreely is super easy to use and is completly free.
