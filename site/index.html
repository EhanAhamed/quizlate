It's free

Unlike other studying tools, Quizfreely has no ads, limits, pop-ups, free trails, or paid features. It's completely free, open source, and it doesn't even require an account!

and open source

Quizfreely is open source. That means it's publicly developed, so everyone can view and contribute to its source code. Quizfreely isn't owned by any company either, so it doesn't need to make money off of its users.
