Privacy policy

Summary

Users who do NOT create/use an account:

  • Can use Quizfreely
  • Will NOT have their data collected

Users with an account:

  • Will have account data (like their email address) collected
  • Will NOT have their account data used for marketing/advertising
  • Can save study sets to their account
  • Will NOT have their study set data used for marketing/advertising

All users:

  • Will have their data processed by Supabase to keep it secure
  • Will NOT have their data sold or shared to anyone else
  • Can email quizfreely@ehan.dev for questions about how their data is used.
  • Can view Quizfreely's source code
  • Can NOT view other users' data

Full privacy policy text

Updated on 2023/07/01

This website, https://quizfreely.ehan.dev ("Quizfreely"), does NOT collect any information ("data") about users who do NOT register ("sign up") for an account with Quizfreely. If a user signs up for an account, Quizfreely collects data about the user and information that makes the user identifiable ("personal information"). The personal information that Quizfreely collects only includes the user's email address.

All data and personal information that is collected by Quizfreely is processed by a third party, Supabase Inc. ("Supabase"). Data that is collected by Quizfreely and processed by Supabase is NOT subject to Supabase's privacy notice ("Supabase's privacy policy").

Users may request details about the disclosure ("sharing") of personal information to third parties. Users may also request for their data and personal information to be deleted. Users can make these requests by contacting quizfreely@ehan.dev.

The data and personal information that is collected by Quizfreely is NOT sold or disclosed ("shared") to any third party that is NOT specified ("listed") in this privacy notice ("privacy policy").

Contact quizfreely@ehan.dev with any questions about this privacy policy.
