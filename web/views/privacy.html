Privacy Policy

Updated September 15, 2024 (2024-09-15)

Quizfreely only stores information or data from users with a Quizfreely account.
Users who use Quizfreely without an account have their data stored on their own devices.
Quizfreely does not collect or store any data from users that do not have an account.

When users create a Quizfreely account, Quizfreely stores their username, display name, and encrypted password to let them log in to and use their account.
When users create a Quizfreely account through Google, Quizfreely stores their Google account id and email address to let them log in to their Quizfreely account through Google.

When users create or interact with content on Quizfreely while logged in to a Quizfreely account, Quizfreely stores content that they post.
Quizfreely lets other users view and interact with public content that users post.

Quizfreely does NOT disclose or sell any user information or data to any other third party or company.
Even when users log in to their Quizfreely account through Google, which is a third party, Quizfreely does not disclose or give any user data to Google.
