Privacy Policy

Updated September 5, 2024 (2024-09-05)

Quizfreely only stores information or data from users with a Quizfreely account.
Quizfreely does not collect or store any information from users who use Quizfreely without an account.

When users create a Quizfreely account, Quizfreely stores their username and encrypted password to let them log in to and use their account.
When users create a Quizfreely account through Google, Quizfreely will store their Google account id and email address to let them log in to their Quizfreely account through Google.

When users create or interact with content on Quizfreely while logged in to a Quizfreely account, Quizfreely stores their user-generated content to let users study, share, and modify their content.

Quizfreely does NOT disclose or sell any user information or data to any third party or company.
Even when users log in to their Quizfreely account through Google, a third party, Quizfreely does not disclose or share any information with Google.
